Moon as natural satellite

Moon is the natural satellite that revolve around the earth . its distance from the earth is 3,84,400 km.it is mainly responsible for the regulation of tides in the ocean . it has no atmosphere of its own and the sun appears to be white from its surface as there is no atmosphere to show the phenomena of sacttering.

Crators on moon

Moon consist of a large number of big as well as small crators that are a result of several collisions during the earliest age of its formation when the universe was in a state of disorder . moreover the gravity on the surface is 1/6th of that on the surface of earth . moreover,the planet is subject to very harmful radiation from the sun as well as cosmic radiations in space,as there is no magnetic field to counter the radiation . because of less gravity one feel loss in weight.

Expedition

The one and the only expedition to land the human on the surface of moon was the appolo 11 mission undertook by united states of america so as to make the dominance in space arena during the years of cold war(1950-1991) so as to win the space wars with USSR.The Apollo 11 mission main mission was to send the mankind on the surface of moon.the astronauts Neil armstrong and edwin aldrin were the only ones who landed ont the surface of moon.
